  • Instant Play/Pause Firstly, press the Spacebar to pause or resume your music playback seamlessly.
  • Skip Back to the Previous Track Tap the Left Arrow key to jump back to the previous track in your playlist quickly.
  • Advance to the Next Song Afterwards, press the Right Arrow key to move to the next track on your playlist instantly.
  • Crank Up the Volume To raise the volume, enhance your listening experience by pressing the Up Arrow key.
  • Dimming the Volume Then, press the Down Arrow key to gently lower the volume, allowing your music to blend into the background seamlessly.
  • Loop Your Favorite Song Press “R” to repeat the song, allowing your favorite tunes to play endlessly.
  • Instant Mute Further, click “M” to quickly mute the song, enabling swift sound adjustments without leaving your current screen.
  • Favorite Management Press the “L” key to easily add or remove a song from your favorites, seamlessly managing your liked tracks.

Getting Started with the Spotify Web Player Extension

After familiarizing yourself with the features and keyboard shortcuts, the next step is to install the Spotify Web Player Chrome extension. Moreover, follow these key steps to integrate this tool into your browser seamlessly:

  • Begin Installation Firstly, start by clicking the “Add to Chrome” button to start the installation process.
  • Approve Installation When prompted, select the “Add Extension” button to continue the installation.
  • Open Extensions Then, locate the “Puzzle” icon on the right side of the Chrome toolbar and click on it.
  • Pin the Extension Click the pin icon next to the extension's name to secure it, ensuring the pin turns blue.
  • Activate the Extension Lastly, finish the installation by playing music on the official Spotify website and managing it with the Mini Player extension.
